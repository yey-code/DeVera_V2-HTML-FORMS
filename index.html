Our Menu

Welcome to Chibogs! Our menu blends Filipino, Italian, Mexican, and French cuisines. Indulge in Filipino favorites like Bulalo, savor Italian classic like Braciolea, dive into Mexican delights like Beef Fajitas, and enjoy French specialties like Beef Bourguignon. Vegetarian options and chef's specials are available for a truly diverse dining experience!

Bulalo

Bulalo is a Filipino beef soup dish made with bone-in beef shanks and marrow bones, simmered for hours until the meat is tender and flavorful, and the broth rich and savory.

Braciole

Braciole is an Italian dish consisting of thinly sliced meat, typically beef, rolled with a savory filling such as breadcrumbs, cheese, herbs, and sometimes cured meats like prosciutto.

Beef Fajitas

Beef fajita is a popular Mexican dish consisting of grilled or pan-seared strips of beef, typically skirt steak or flank steak, seasoned with spices such as chili powder, cumin, and garlic. The beef is often cooked alongside onions and bell peppers, adding flavor and texture to the dish..

Bœuf bourguignon

Bœuf bourguignon is a classic French dish originating from the Burgundy region. It features tender beef slowly braised in red wine, typically Burgundy wine, along with onions, carrots, mushrooms, and herbs.

Vegetarian Cuisine

From hearty vegetable stir-fries and vibrant salads to creamy pasta dishes and flavorful curries, our vegetarian menu offers something for every palate. Indulge in our signature Mushroom Risotto, savor the freshness of our Garden Salad, or dive into the exotic flavors of our Vegetable Curry.

Special Cuisine

Indulge in the Scandinavian delight of smorgasbord, featuring a lavish spread of cold and hot dishes. Dive into the rich flavors of curanto, a traditional Chilean dish cooked underground, combining an assortment of seafood, meats, and vegetables. Craving something exotic? Try our mouthwatering doner kebab, a Turkish-inspired dish featuring succulent meat cooked on a vertical rotisserie and served in warm flatbread with fresh vegetables and sauces.
